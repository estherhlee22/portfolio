KUTIKATI

E-COMMERCE STORE

BRANDING & WEB DESIGN

APPROACH & PROCESS

Kutikati is an organic baby clothing company whose aim is to sell products that care for the well-being of both the baby and the environment. Kutikati sets itself apart from its competitors by highlighting the product itself and the benefits of it.

After surveying and interviewing mothers, Kutikati's target audience, I concluded that mothers are highly conscious of two things: their time and the health and well-being of their child. With that idea, I decided that Kutikati's e-commerce store should focus on showcasing the products, which in and of itself meet the needs of health-conscious mothers. In addition, by minimizing the layout to show just what is necessary, Kutikati's e-commerce store allows for a smooth and fast shopping experience for busy mothers.

RESEARCH

I compiled a list of user stories and features after looking through various e-commerce sites and using them as examples. Typically, many clothing sites, especially ones for babies, have multiple subcategories and even have options to specify their search. In my original list of user stories, I considered including these options in Kutikati's site as well. However given the company size of Kutikati, I decided that these options are not necessary and only complicate the experience of the users

DESIGN

I researched many clothing brand logos and saw that boutique-like clothing brands have a text-based logo instead of an iconic one. The nature of Kutikati’s product focuses on a friendly tone, both to individual families and the environment, so I felt that it should not market itself as a massive enterprise with a strong iconic logo. Instead I opted for a clean and well typeset text-based logo that could easily go on tags and labels.

The color palette was drawn from classic baby colors such as light pink and faint beige, but also highlighted the organic aspect of the brand through the forest green. The combination of the colors created a synergy of the playful tone of Kutikati’s clothing style and patterns.
