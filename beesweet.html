BEESWEET LEMONADE

PRODUCT BRANDING & SHOWCASE

APPROACH & PROCESS

BeeSweet Lemonade is a beverage company started by an 11-year-old girl, Mikaila Ulmer. This project attempted to showcase the uniqueness of the product’s founder and her age as well as highlight the 1940’s recipe that Ulmer received from her great-grandmother on a fully responsive site.

The challenge in this process was finding a way to present a lot of different information without overbearing site visitors. There are many unique features about this lemonade product, but without proper organization of the site’s content, I knew it would easy for new visitors to miss out on why BeeSweet is different form other lemonades.

Because the primary factor that sets BeeSweet apart from its competitors is its 11-year-old founder, I wanted to highlight its uniqueness by putting the story behind BeeSweet in the very beginning. Within “Our Story,” BeeSweet Lemonade introduces its founder as well as its 1940’s recipe, highlighting two unique aspects of the product.

DESIGN

The logo needed to be applicable to both the lemonade bottle and other other promotional material, including the product showcase website. Honey is a unique ingredient in BeeSweet’s lemonade and so a hexagon shape is used as a reference to honeycombs. It’s a simple solution to mark the brand on bottles, websites and flyers.

I used a primary color palette to highlight the youth of BeeSweet’s founder. The hues of the red, blue and yellow were intentionally selected to give off a retro vibe, as the recipe is from the 1940s.
