BEESWEET LEMONADE

PRODUCT BRANDING & SHOWCASE

APPROACH & PROCESS

BeeSweet Lemonade is a beverage company started by an 11-year-old girl, Mikaila Ulmer. This project attempted to showcase the uniqueness of the product’s founder and her age as well as highlight the healthy recipe that Ulmer received from her great-grandmother’s 1940 recipe on a fully responsive site.

The challenge in this process was finding a way to present a lot of different information without overbearing visitors to the site. There are many unique features about this lemonade product, but without proper organization of the site’s content, I knew it would be easy for new visitors to miss out on why BeeSweet is different from other lemonades.

Because the primary factor sets BeeSweet apart from its competitors is its 11-year-old founder, I felt that the best way to highlight this is to put the story behind BeeSweet in the very beginning. Within “Our Story,” BeeSweet Lemonade introduces its founder as well as its 1940 recipe, highlighting 2 unique aspects of the product.

DESIGN

LOGO & PRODUCT DESIGN

The logo needed to be applicable for both the bottle and other promotional material including the product showcase website. The logo merges a hexagon shape for honeycombs, which represents the unique ingredient in this lemonade and a lemon. It’s a simple solution to mark the brand on bottles, websites and flyers.
