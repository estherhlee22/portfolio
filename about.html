Hello. I'm Esther.

I received my formal education from the University of Southern California where I graduated with a BA in Fine Arts-Design, but my understanding of design radically changed after spending one year in Haiti living and working amongst one of the poorest and most poverty-stricken population in the world. As I was faced with issues ranging from homelessness to unemployment to lack of education, I saw myself and the way I think of design change from simple aesthetics to people-focused problem solving.

I am a creative problem solver, and I recognize the potential design has to impact communities, organizations, cities and companies. If you’d like to work with me, feel free to drop me a message!
