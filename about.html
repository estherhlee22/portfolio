Hello!

After graduating from the University of Southern California with a degree in Fine Arts- Design, I spent a year living in Port-Au-Prince, Haiti where the 2010 earthquake left the nation in ruins. As I worked with some of the poorest and poverty-stricken population in the world, I saw myself and the way I view design change from simple aesthetics to people-focused problem solving.

I am a creative problem solver, compassionate thinker and constant learner. I recognize the potential design and technology have to impact businesses, communities, organizations and cities, and I am eager to grow as a designer to make change.

If you’d like to work with me, feel free to drop me a message.
